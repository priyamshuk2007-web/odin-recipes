Tiramisu

STiramisu is a classic Italian dessert consisting of coffee-soaked ladyfingers layered with a whipped mixture of eggs, sugar, and mascarpone cheese, then topped with cocoa powder. The dessert's name translates to "pick me up" or "cheer me up," a nod to its energizing espresso content

Ingredients

  • ladyfingers

  • Mascarpone

  • coffee

  • Heavy whipped cream

  • Granulated sugar

  • Vanilla extract

  • Cocoa powder

Now when we have the needed ingredients Lets start the cooking process

  1. Mix creamy filling. Beat the mascarpone, cream, sugar, and vanilla together until stiff peaks

  2. Dip lady fingers. Add the espresso and liqueur (if using) to a shallow bowl and dip the lady fingers on both sides (don't let them soakâ€“just a quick dip!)

  3. Layer mascarpone. Smooth a layer of the mascarpone/whipped cream mixture on top of the lady fingers

  4. Repeat. Add another layer of lady fingers (dipped in coffee and liqueur) and another layer of cheese mixture. Dust with cocoa powder

  5. Tiramisu is even better when made in advance, allowing the flavors to blend! It will keep in the refrigerator for 2 to 3 days.
